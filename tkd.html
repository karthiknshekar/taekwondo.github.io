Two-A-Day Workout Timetable

Day 1: Strength and Conditioning

Morning Session Evening Session
  • Squats: 5 sets x 6-8 reps
  • Bench Press: 4 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Pull-Ups: 4 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Deadlifts: 4 sets x 6 reps
  • Plank with Shoulder Taps: 4 sets x 1 minute
  • High Intensity Interval Training: 30s on/30s off for 20 mins
  • Taekwondo Techniques: 40 mins
  • Sparring Drills: 20-30 mins
  • Cool Down: Active stretching for 15 mins

Day 2: Speed and Agility

Morning Session Evening Session
  • Dynamic Warm-up: 15 minutes
  • Agility Drills: 20 minutes
  • Depth Jumps: 4 sets x 8 reps
  • Burpees: 4 sets x 10 reps
  • 50-meter Sprints: 10 sets
  • Taekwondo Techniques: 40 minutes
  • Bag Work: 4 rounds x 3 minutes
  • High-Intensity Cardio (Tabata): 5 sets x 3 minutes
  • Cool Down: Static stretching for 20 minutes

Day 3: Active Recovery

Morning or Afternoon
  • Light Jogging or Walking: 20-30 minutes
  • basic kicking: 30 minutes
  • Massage: 15 minutes

Day 4: Power and Plyometrics

Morning Session Evening Session
  • Box Jumps: 5 sets x 5 reps
  • Power Cleans: 4 sets x 6 reps
  • Push-Ups: 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Plank : 4 sets x 1 minute
  • Taekwondo Techniques: 45 minutes
  • Sparring Drills: 30 minutes
  • Sprint Intervals: 8 x 100 meters
  • Cool Down: Active stretching for 20 minutes

Day 5: Endurance and Cardio

Morning Session Evening Session
  • Long-distance Run: 5-8 kilometers
  • HIIT: 4 sets x 4 minutes
  • Bag Work: 3 rounds x 3 minutes
  • Core exercises: 3 sets x 15 reps
  • Taekwondo Techniques: 40 minutes
  • Sparring Drills: 20-30 minutes
  • Cool Down: Active stretching for 15 minutes

Day 6: Flexibility and Mobility

Morning Session Evening Session
  • Dynamic Stretching Routine: 10 minutes
  • Basic Kicking: 30 minutes
  • Taekwondo-specific Stretches: 20 minutes
  • Massage: 15 minutes
  • Light Kicking Drills for Flexibility: 15 minutes
  • Taekwondo Techniques: 30 minutes
  • Cool Down: Static Stretching for 20 minutes

Day 7: Rest

Allow your body to recover completely. Focus on hydration and proper nutrition.
